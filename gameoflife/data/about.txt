Main Guide:

    Start - Starts or continues the currently loaded simulation.

    Stop - Pauses the currently loaded simulation.

    New - Initiates a new simulation.

    Select - Below the select button is the selection field for different
             pattern types. Click a pattern and load it by clicking Select.
    Quit - Quits the application.


Settings Guide:

    Themes - Choose different color themes.

    Cells - Choose different character representations of the cells.

    Resolution - Toggle between Fullscreen/Windowed mode. On windowed mode,
                 choose between different resolutions.
    Framerate - Choose the framerate of the simulation.

    Submit Settings - After choosing settings, click this to submit them.
